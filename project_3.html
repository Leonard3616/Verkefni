Project 3

3D printing and scanning in 3D project

Introduction

This third project will consist in 2 parts:

The first part of the proyect to design a model to 3D print. The wheigt needs to be 100g max. The printer that we will use is Prusa MK3+. We will use Fusion 360 for this.

The second part It will consist of scanning a 3D object with the use of an app for example photogrammetry.

3D printing

For this first part of the project I started to look online about what I could desing or do. After being thinking about many ideas that I could do. I decided to design a headphone stand. This is the headset holder that I used as reference for mine, in which contained measurements that I used in the design: Headset holder.

Here I started to add a bit of what I would like to my headset holder to look like. Thought it was a really good touch to split it in 2.

After taking measurements of my headphone, like the curvature on top and also using the measurements obtained from the headset holder that I found online. Everything started just to come in the right place. Didn't take so much time to draw it.

Then I exported it as an STL. document to be able to open it in the program PrusaSlicer. This program is the one that we will use to prepare our model to 3D printe it. When I was done changing the configurations in PrusaSlicer I realize that my deign was to big for it and also that was heavier then 100g. So that I had to modify my design, and I did.

While modifing it I also want it to do a mark on it, so I wrote my initials on it. And this is how it looked at the end.

When I imported in PrusaSlicer was still around 2g over the limit, but Hafliði said that it was okay.

It took 7h1m to print just as said in PrusaSlicer and I used a 15% of fill wich I knew it will make it very firm. We had also to upload the document from PrusaSlicer to a SDcard because that's what Prusa MK3+ use to read the document to print.
